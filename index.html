The Idea

We gave work
to nouns.
We want it back.

We talk about "the economy," "work," "the market" as if they have wills of their own. They don't. People do. Nature does. Treating abstractions as agents — as things that act upon us — is how we stopped noticing who actually makes the decisions.

This is not a site about jobs. It is about recovering the full range of what human beings do when they contribute to the world — and the language to name it.

This is not
Career advice · productivity culture · futures forecasting · hustle
This is
An excavation · a gathering of communities practicing differently · language for what you've already sensed
