What we make

Three kinds
of conversation.

◎
Thinker interviews
Long-form conversations with researchers, writers, and practitioners. Ideas that take time to unfold. The kind of interview that assumes you can handle complexity.
eg. Nora Bateson on Warm Data · Kathi Weeks on refusal · a co-op founder on what governance actually costs
◐
Field dispatches
Personal accounts of navigating work in transition. Layoffs, pivots, burnout, reinvention. Not advice. Just honest testimony from people who've been through it.
eg. What it actually felt like to be laid off at 47 · Building a worker co-op from scratch · Leaving consulting to grow food
∿
Topic deep dives
A host-driven episode examining one idea in depth — like the Team Human format, with a car-guy's pleasure in taking things apart to see how they work.
eg. The full story of how tipping started · What Maslow actually stole from the Blackfoot · Why bullshit jobs exist

Want to be
on the show?

We're looking for people who've lived something interesting — not just people who've written about it. If you've navigated a significant work transition, built something unconventional, or have a body of research that belongs in this conversation, we want to hear from you.

We especially want: people with firsthand experience of cooperative structures, care workers, organizers, anyone who's been through a layoff and come out with a different frame, researchers doing work that doesn't fit standard disciplines, practitioners of indigenous or non-Western approaches to livelihood.
