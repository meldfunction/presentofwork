Meta Guilds

Learning communities that operate in the liminal spaces between skill development, spiritual practice, and systems transformation

What Makes Meta Guilds Different

Unlike traditional guilds that focus on specific trades, meta guilds recognize that in the present of work, developing capacity and developing consciousness are inseparable processes.

What is a Meta Guild?

Meta guilds are learning communities that transcend traditional skill-based training by operating in the liminal spaces where personal development, community building, and systems transformation intersect.

Modern Meta Guilds

We've evolved beyond traditional guild models to develop expertise in transcontextual pattern recognition, facilitating emergence in complex systems, gift economy and regenerative livelihood design, and indigenous wisdom integration.

Learning Through Liminal Practice

8-12 week intensive programs with ongoing integration, embodied practice sessions, mentorship webs rather than hierarchical relationships, and real-world application in community transformation projects.

Ecosystem Integration

Meta guild participants become nodes in our living network with deep integration with Soil/Commons, development as conscious Knots in webs of relationship, and active participation in Social Web transformation projects.

Financial Reality & Accessibility

Being transparent about costs and our commitment to ensuring financial barriers don't prevent participation

Gift Economy Pricing

Standard: $75-150/month based on guild intensity

Scholarships: Reduced or waived fees - no one turned away

Work-Trade: Contribute skills in exchange

At-Cost Labs: Charged only for space/food costs

Sustainability for Facilitators

Guild facilitators receive support through participant contributions, commons fund subsidies, and consulting revenue sharing.

No facilitator should bear financial burden for making guilds accessible.

Value Beyond Cost

Ongoing access to community support network for years after completion, potential pathways to consulting work, and foundation for contributing as facilitator in future guilds.
